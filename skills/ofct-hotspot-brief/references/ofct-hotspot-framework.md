# OFCT Hotspot Framework

## Core Positioning
Column name: 用 OFCT 看热点.

Purpose: use current events as entry points so more people see OFCT as a tool for understanding the world they already care about.

Do not start from theory. Start from the public question raised by the event.

## Topic Selection
Good topics:
- Popular public events: sports, cultural phenomena, education, work, relationships, creators, platforms, social norms, city life, weather and travel disruptions, public rituals, major ceremonies, and collective emotions.
- AI products, chips, robots, agents, wearables, brain-computer interfaces.
- Major science and technology breakthroughs.
- Viral social emotions: anxiety, relationship debates, youth choices, workplace conflict.
- Cultural phenomena that reveal collective relationship fields.

Avoid or handle gently:
- fresh deaths, disasters, violent crimes, medical emergencies.
- highly partisan conflicts unless user explicitly asks.
- topics where current facts are unclear.
- topics that only interest a narrow technical audience, unless the user asks for technology-focused posts.

## OFCT Lens Map
### 生命接口
Use when tools change perception, feedback, action, body, memory, attention, or cognition.
Hook: “这不是多了一个工具，而是打开世界的方式变了。”

### 关系场
Use when AI/platforms/institutions/teams rearrange people’s positions.
Hook: “最先变的不是工作，而是人和人的位置。”

For sports or competitions, use 关系场 when a team's collective trust, pressure resistance, and shared rhythm matter more than any single star.
Hook: “冠军不是最亮的个体，而是高压时不散的整体。”

### 文明接口
Use for infrastructure-level technology or major breakthroughs.
Hook: “它不是一个产品，而是文明读取世界的新接口。”

### 时代页码
Use for collective anxiety, generational change, macro trends, large public events, and moments where society seems to turn a page.
Hook: “这不是个人背景，而是时代条件写进了体验之书。”

### 体验之书
Use for individual life choices, identity, career, relationships, family, education, or repeated everyday scenes.
Hook: “你经历的不是孤立事件，而是某一页被点亮。”

### 命运节点 / 显影节点
Use for breakthroughs, iconic products, public figures, competitions, sudden turning points, or events that reveal a hidden structure.
Hook: “真正重要的不是这个人有多神，而是他让一个趋势显影。”

## Copy Formula
1. One-sentence hook tied to the hot topic.
2. Say what people usually think it is.
3. Reframe using OFCT.
4. Explain one mechanism in plain language.
5. End with a memorable question or sentence.

## Creator Voice Formula
When writing as the OFCT creator, use a clear first-person structure:
1. “我用 OFCT 看这个事件，判断是...”
2. “很多人会先看...”
3. “但我更看...”
4. “表层是..., 深层是...”
5. “所以我的判断是...”

Keep the voice firm, direct, and exploratory. Do not become motivational or preachy.

## Image Prompt Formula
Vertical 9:16. Main title at top. Main scene in middle. One speech bubble or AI popup. Minimal labels. Use bright sci-fi manga style. Prefer relationship lines, interface layers, page corridors, transparent maps, clean office/lab/city/sport scenes.

## Fixed Characters
最近又胖了: chubby yellow bear host, always the protagonist and presenter. Retain the bear head and rainbow cap with lemon-slice ear decorations when visual consistency matters. Clothing may change by topic.

四千: silver tabby cat assistant, skeptical and sharp. Often challenges abstract claims.

草莓: silver tabby cat assistant, softer and emotionally perceptive. Often translates the idea into daily feeling.

AI interface: optional tool, screen, popup, or holographic assistant. It should not replace the three protagonists.

## Title Patterns
- “AI 改变的不是 X，是 Y”
- “这不是 X，而是 Y”
- “热点背后，其实是 X 在变化”
- “用 OFCT 看：为什么 X 会发生？”
- “当 X 出现，人的 Y 会被改写”
- “冠军不是 X，而是 Y”
- “不是谁 X，而是谁 Y”

## Quality Bar
The output should be usable for posting after light editing. Avoid generic motivational content. The OFCT angle must be specific and tied to the event.

The reader should feel: “我本来只是刷到一个热点，但这套解释让我多看见了一层。”
